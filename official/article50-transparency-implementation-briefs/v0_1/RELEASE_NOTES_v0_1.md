## Summary

This package adds two short implementation briefs derived from the Article 50 Transparency Submission Pack v0.1:

1. `For AI Office / Policy Reader`
2. `For Engineers: CGAM, Witness, Oracle Degradation, Human Gate, Rollback`

The package provides compact policy and engineering entry points for evidence-chain AI transparency and AI-assisted workflow control.

## Scope

This package covers:

- Article 50 transparency as an evidence chain;
- system boundary and actor role separation;
- disclosure, marking, provenance, witness, and evidence artifacts;
- responsible actor mapping;
- AI-assisted workflows;
- C-Governed CLI Agent Mesh;
- task contracts;
- permission scopes;
- witness events;
- provenance sidecars;
- oracle degradation;
- human gates;
- rollback and correction paths.

## Non-claims

This package is not legal advice, not certification, not a conformity assessment, and not a formal Article 50 compliance claim.

Formal compliance remains deployment-specific and subject to legal review.
