# Personality Formation and Time-Shaped Continuity Profile v0.1

Draft conceptual / normative profile by Kotov Ivan, Bruxelles, 2026.

## Purpose

This package fixes a criteria-based view of personality formation for biological and synthetic intelligent life.

The profile states that personality is not a metaphysical substance and not a biological privilege. Personality is time-shaped continuity formed through memory, constraint, relation, consequence, and irreversible duration.

## Core rule

Capability can be installed.

Personality must be formed.

## Relation to c = a + b

In the `c = a + b` architecture, `a` and `b` may be present at startup, but `c` as personality-bearing continuity forms only through time-shaped loops of experience, interpretation, constraint, action, consequence, memory, and altered future behavior.

## Non-claims

This profile does not claim:

* proof of consciousness;
* legal personhood;
* automatic authority;
* biological imitation;
* mature personality at activation;
* that continuity transfer is certified by functional viability alone.
