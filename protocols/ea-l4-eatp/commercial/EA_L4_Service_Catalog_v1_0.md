## **1. Positioning**

This catalog defines **practical services** built on:

* EATP (Experience Artifact Training Protocol)
* L4 Reality-Bound Architecture
* Witness-based auditability

It targets organizations where:

* AI decisions carry **financial, legal, or operational consequence**
* post-hoc explainability is insufficient
* regulatory exposure is non-trivial

---

## **2. Core Offering Structure**

Three primary services:

```text id="3ydj5v"
S1 — AI Act Gap Assessment
S2 — L4 Witness & EA Audit Layer
S3 — Sovereign & Confidential AI Architecture
```

---

# **S1 — AI Act Gap Assessment**

## **Objective**

Evaluate whether an AI system is:

* auditable
* traceable
* compliant-ready

---

## **Scope**

* decision lifecycle analysis
* training data provenance review
* execution boundary validation
* oversight feasibility (Article 14)
* transparency gaps (Article 50)

---

## **Deliverables**

* structured gap report
* risk classification (low / medium / high)
* mapping to AI Act requirements
* prioritized remediation actions

---

## **Method**

* architecture inspection
* pipeline tracing
* synthetic vs real data separation analysis
* decision reconstruction tests

---

## **Outcome**

Client understands:

* where compliance is assumed vs provable
* where auditability fails structurally

---

## **Constraint**

Assessment is:

* non-invasive
* does not require source code modification

---

---

# **S2 — L4 Witness & EA Audit Layer**

## **Objective**

Introduce:

* verifiable execution trace
* experience-grounded auditability

---

## **Scope**

* integration of witness envelopes
* EA capture layer (minimal deployment)
* append-only audit structure
* provenance chain setup

---

## **Deliverables**

* L4 Witness-compatible audit layer
* EA capture schema implementation
* audit trace validation procedure
* verification guidelines

---

## **Method**

* attach witness layer to execution boundary
* define EA capture points
* implement hash-chain linkage
* validate tamper-evidence

---

## **Outcome**

System gains:

* deterministic traceability
* evidence-backed decision history
* audit-ready structure

---

## **Constraint**

This layer:

* does NOT alter core model behavior
* operates at execution + logging boundary

---

---

# **S3 — Sovereign & Confidential AI Architecture**

## **Objective**

Design AI systems that are:

* privacy-preserving
* locally anchored
* federated when required

---

## **Scope**

* hybrid local/cloud architecture
* entity-based orchestration (c = a + b)
* confidential computation patterns
* federation (optional)

---

## **Deliverables**

* architecture blueprint
* deployment model
* data flow design
* privacy & control boundaries

---

## **Method**

* identify sensitive flows
* separate execution domains
* define privilege boundaries
* integrate EA pipeline where needed

---

## **Outcome**

Client obtains:

* reduced dependency on external providers
* controlled data exposure
* scalable, compliant-ready architecture

---

## **Constraint**

Architecture is:

* modular
* compatible with existing infrastructure
